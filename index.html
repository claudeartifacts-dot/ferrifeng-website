About the Artist

The nature is an artist's GURU

I am Ferri Feng, an abstract painter based in Indonesia. For more than twenty years I have worked in aviation — a world of data, precision and procedure. Painting is where that precision learns to let go.

My works begin where the horizon blurs: the deep blues of the Java Sea, the drama of a storm cloud seen from the flight deck, the stillness above the weather. Each canvas carries a word I hold close — sumeleh, the Javanese art of surrendering without giving up.

Signature Series

Cockpit to Canvas

A continuing series translating what aviation professionals see — and passengers miss — into abstract paintings: cumulonimbus towers, city lights at 2 a.m., golden hour above the clouds.

Follow the series on Instagram @ferrifeng — each episode pairs real footage from altitude with the finished canvas.
